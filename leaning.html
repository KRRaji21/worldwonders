Leaning Tower of Pisa

The Leaning Tower of Pisa (or) simply the tower of pisa is the companile (or) freestanding bell tower of the cathedral of the italian city if pisa known worldwide for its indendent tilt.

Leaning Tower of Pisa Facts:

  • In italian Leaning Tower of Pisa is Torre Pendente di pisa

  • The Tower weighs about 14,500 tons.

  • There are approximately 294 steps on north side and 296 steps on the South side of the tower.

  • The Leaning Tower of Pisa took 344 years to build beginning in August 1173.

  • Inside the tower there are 7 bells are located on the 8thfloor.
