Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus (or) tomb of mausoleum was a tomb built between 353 and 350BC at Halicarnassus for mausoleum.A satrap in the persian empire and his sister-wife Artemisia II of caria

Mausoleum at Halicarnassus Facts:

  • The word mausoleum originates from the name Mausolus.

  • Mausoleus is wife Artemisia was also his sister.Mausoleum at Halicarnassus was common in these days for rulers to many their sister.

  • Artemisia hired famous Greek Artist to built the tomb

  • Most of the Mausoleum was made of marble.Mausoleum at Halicarnassus was surrounded by a courtyard.
