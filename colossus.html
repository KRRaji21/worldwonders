Colossus of Rhodes

The Colossus of Rhodes was a Statue of greek titan-god of the sun helios,erected in the city of rhodes,on the greek Island of the same name,by Charas of Lindos in 280BC.

Colossus of Rhodes Facts:

  • The Rhodians also used Brase and iron from the eqipment left behind the statue.

  • The Colossus of Rhodes 50 feet high white marble pedestal.

  • Both Colossus of Rhodes and Statue of Liberty were built as " Symbol of freedom".

  • Construction of Colossus of Rhodes took12 years to complete.

  • Colossus of Rhodes was destroyed in a earthquake in 226BC.Colossus of Rhodes was reconstructed.
