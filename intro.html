Wonders of the World

* From Left to Right ,Top to Bottom:Wonders of the World was an initiative started in 2000 to choose Wonders of the World from a selection of 200 existing monuments. The popularity poll was led by Canadian-Swiss Bernard Weber and organized by the Wonders of the World Foundation based in Zurich, Swizerland. This Wonders are Catagorized based on the duration.



Catagories of Wonders of the World:-
  • Ancient Wonders of the World
  • Medieval Wonders of the World
  • Modern Wonders of the World
