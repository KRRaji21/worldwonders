Christ the Redeemer

The Christ the Redeemer is an art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro,Brazil,created by French sculptor Paul Landowski and built by the Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa,in collabration with the French engineer Albert Caquat.

Christ the Redeemer Facts:

  • It took around 9 Years from 1922-1931 to make this thing a wonder statue mainly due to its cliff like location.

  • This is the 3rdtallest statue of Jesus in the world after Poland's Jesus and Bolivia's Cristo de la Concordia.

  • The statue was made with the purpose to show thatChrist loves all,spread peace and to display the devotion of the city towards the almighty.

  • A special kind of rock was used.The stone was especially imported from Sweden.
