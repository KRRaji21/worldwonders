Catacombs of Kom El Shaqafa

The Catacombs of Kom El Shaqafa is a historical archacological site located in Alexandria,Egypt and is considered of the wonder of Middle Ages.

Catacombs of Kom El Shaqafa Facts:

  • The Catacombs of Kom El Shaqafa was used as a burial ground from the 2nd to 4th Centuries A.D..

  • On the first level there is a hall way that leak to room called"The hall of Caracalla".

  • The Catacombs of Kom El Shaqafa means "Mound of Shards".

  • The Catacombs of Kom El Shaqafa reach 100 feet into the ground into solid Rocks
